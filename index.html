QuotePlease

Quotesplease is an application that provides daily commuters with a chance to freshen up their minds with a plaform for which they can view, interact, and share quotes from books, movies, lyrics, and more

More

Target User

QuotePlease targets any lovers of any medium (books, poems, movies, lyrics, etc.) who are too busy to have enough time to consume their favorite shows and books. In this sense, commuters are the primary target user.

Features

Features of QuotePlease include: Quote View, Quote Organization, Quote Recommendation, Quote Library, Quote Share, Quote Comment, Quote Context, and more
